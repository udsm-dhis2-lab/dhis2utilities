E.2 Preparation & Administration of Injectable Artesunate for Severe Malaria (Observation/Simulation)

Instructions: Observe health care provider who regularly treats severe malaria patients while they prepare and administer injectable artesunate through observation of an actual patient interaction. If no patient present, request health care provider to demonstrate as a simulation. Do not interrupt unless the patient is critically ill and needs immediate intervention/attention or if the practice of the clinician will put the patient in danger. If you are not able to observe, please fill out the reason at the end of this observation and complete the knowledge section below.

Step 1: Gets the contents correctly out of the package (check for the availability of the following three items: sodium bicarbonate ampoule, Artesunate vials and 5% Dextrose/0.9 saline ampoule)
Step 2: Draws 1ml of sodium bicarbonate into the syringe
Step 3: Adds 1ml of sodium bicarbonate into Artesunate vials (60mg)
Step 4: Shakes for 2-3 minutes
Step 5: Waits until completely dissolve and solution is clear
Step 6: Draws into syringe 5% Dextrose/0.9 saline (2ml for IM; 5ml for IV)
Step 7: Adds the contents into Artesunate vial
Step 8: Shakes for 30 seconds
Step 9: Determines the dose (round up to the nearest ml)
Step 10: Draws the required dose into the syringe
Step 11: Administered according to dilution
Step 12: Discarded all the syringes and needles into safety box immediately after use
Step 13: Records the procedure in the injection register
Step 14: Absence of any diluted Artesunate not used within one hour from reconstitution in the injection area
SUB-SCORE: E.2 Inj. Artesunate: Observation/Simulation
